ABSTRACT

The project aims at developing a completely Integrated Application designed to cater

to every administrative requirement in the proper functioning of Hostel Management.

It will be a tool for managing resources, gathering essential data, conversion of this

data into information, and making the information available to right person at right

time for informed decision making. This includes receiving specifications of student,

confirmation of allotment and operating multiple entities without compromising on

the integration of the application. Thus the project aims at improved service and

consistent reviewing of status of the hostel information at any point of time.

INTRODUCTION

1.1 The Client Organization

Kamla Nehru Boys hostel manages the hostel for students of different courses in IIPS.

It manages 40 rooms, divided in 2 wings A and B. It assigns a room to 2 students

only. Fees payment is done through bank; bank receipt record is then maintained. It

also has Store to issue and retrieve utilities. Guest record is also maintained in KNBH.

1.2 Problem Definition

The following points describe the problem domain at the client organization:-

 Non integration of all records of the hostel.

 Most of the bills are written on a paper and are sent to accounts department for

final billing, which gives scope of human error.

 All the information in the system is maintained on the paper. This results in

wastage of time and energy.

 Due to manual system, fine collection is not done timely.

 There is no Student Identification code.

 Records may have to be edited or re-entered adding to the cost.

 In case of leaving hostel, updation of records become complex, and check like,

fees payment, is also complex.
